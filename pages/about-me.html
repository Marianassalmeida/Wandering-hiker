Hi There, Welcome!

I'm Mariana, the trail-loving adventurer behind this blog. I've always been drawn to the outdoors—the crunch of gravel under my boots, the crisp mountain air, and the sense of peace that comes with exploring uncharted trails.

This blog is my way of sharing my passion for hiking, from breathtaking summit views to the little joys of a quiet forest walk. Whether you're a seasoned trekker or just lacing up your boots for the first time, you'll find trail sugestions, and stories that (Hopefully!) inspire your next adventure.

When I'm not out chasing sunsets or poring over maps, you'll usually find me by a campfire, planning my next escape into the wild. Let's hit the trail together— One step at a time.

Feel free to sugest me a trail!
